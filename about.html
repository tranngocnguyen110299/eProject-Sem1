About Us

The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park

Our Story

Fun Zone more than 20 years ago was a wild, sparsely populated land with a small stream hiding itself in the deserted nature.



1985

A son of the American homeland with intense feelings in him urged him intensely: "To arouse the lifeline of this land." With dreams, ambitions, creativity and determination, Mr. Jeref has started to build "Fun Zone Forest Camp" with the long-term purpose of hiding inside: "Forest camp is a premise for a big tourist area in the future".
The initial area of the Farm was only 6,400 m2 of unused land, just a small python farm. In the following years, the farm organizes animal husbandry in the way of export industry and at the same time cultivates both rare and precious species and cultivates fruit trees. From 1989 to 1990, Lam Trai produced sawn timber for export, then produced high-end handicrafts. Fun Zone Forest Products are trusted by domestic and foreign customers. In particular, the world CITES organization has approved and protected the farms and exports of reptile animals. Sometimes the python camp here is up to 12,000, so someone calls Fun Zone Forest Farm a python camp.




1991

Investment capital already exists, the construction of a large tourist area to serve the cultural and spiritual life of the people is an essential necessity. From here the construction project The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park was built and formed, the construction items turn up. From a legendary stream, Fun Zone has become The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park, daily curving between beautiful, majestic buildings, as a backdrop for the beautiful Fun zone.



1995

On the occasion of the Lunar New Year, The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park officially opens to welcome visitors from all over the country to visit. The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park has created very large, unique, meaningful works that evoke in every tourist who set foot to the amusement park.


Over 20 years of construction and development, Fun Zone has become a leading amusement park in the world, while affirming its position in the region and the world. With an area of 200 hectares, over 190 buildings and entertainment models, diverse and abundant. Every year, Fun Zone welcomes about 48 million visitors.

Nowadays

Fun Zone with the motto "Always innovating - Always growing". Every year, The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park offers from 2 to 4 new projects to serve visitors, while constantly training and professional staff according to ISO 9001 - 2000 standard

Strategy

vision

Not stopping at the record achieved, Fun Zone Amusement Park has been and continues to expand and upgrade modern entertainment services, follow the world trend, meeting all needs. and the tastes of the visitors. Especially in 2019, Fun Zone will spend 80 hectares to build the Race Course. The 5-in-1 integration project includes horse racing, dog racing, Go Kart racing, motor racing and jet skiing. The track has a length of about 1.800m. In the first stage, the stand is designed to accommodate 25,000 seats and will be upgraded and expanded in the second stage to 60,000 seats. This project promises to open new prospects for Fun Zone in particular and the entertainment industry in general in the coming time.
