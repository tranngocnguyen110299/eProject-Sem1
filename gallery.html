Sushi

$3.45

Sushi is cut into slices, used immediately after being served, and served with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger. When used, put the whole piece in your mouth to fully feel the taste in each circle. The strange taste of rice mixed with vinegar, the sweet and cool taste of raw fish and the pungent taste of wasabi rushed to the nose. The most common ingredients are tuna, squid and shrimp served with cucumber, pickled radish and sweet egg. Great!
Sashimi

$4.55

Sashimi is a traditional Japanese dish whose main ingredients are mainly fresh seafood. Seafood used to make Sashimi must have "sashimi standard", be caught by separate tools, then be treated immediately according to special procedures to ensure the freshness of each piece of Sashimi. Some types of seafood are: salmon, tuna, mackerel, basa fish, puffer fish, squid, octopus, sea shrimp, ensuring great taste!
Shabu-shabu

$10.2

Shabu-shabu is also a hotpot but the main ingredient is beef. Fresh, quality slices of beef are sliced quite skillfully, diners turn these slices of meat into hot water in turn and eat it while still slightly will ensure the taste of the dish. Shabu-shabu hotpot has a sweet taste, clear water and is often served with cabbage, seaweed, shiitake mushrooms and some healthy, healthy ingredients.
Udon noodles

$22.9

Udon is made from flour, salt and water, can be eaten hot or cold but hot food is still more attractive. Udon noodles are white, big and chewy. The broth has a strange and characteristic taste - light salty and sweet. Udon is very suitable for fast food.
Tempura

$18.8

Tempura is a dish of Japanese cuisine including seafood, vegetables, and tubers of fried bread submerged in oil. Tempura can be said to be a typical dish of the land of the rising sun. Although it was born after Sushi, Tempura brought a new flavor and flavor, so it was especially loved by Japanese people. Come here to enjoy it!
Yakitori

$33.9

Yakitori is one of the Japanese delicacies loved by many tourists who come to Japan. To make this dish, the chef used chicken to bake. Chicken is marinated with salt and a typical sauce called "tare". Tare is made from soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar with a recipe that creates a balance between salinity and sweetness. In addition, ginger is added for a strong aroma, subtly add a bit of the fatty taste of sesame oil and sweet honey, creating a very characteristic flavor, attracting visitors the first time to enjoy.
Naan bread

$2.3

This is a typical type of Indian bread. Naan bread is made of two very basic ingredients, flour and dry yeast, but the processing is quite elaborate, first chefs mold the cakes into thin flakes and then put them around the pot to bake. Because of the rather light taste, Naan bread is eaten with cheese. Since then, it has become a great dish.
Dosa cake

$3.9

Dosa is a roll of rice paper made from a mixture of rice flour and black lentils. With the advantages of easy to make, the material is not picky, cheap price, this cake is popular in any corner of India. In "Fun Zone", Dosa has no personality and should be served with a dipping sauce, which is spicy curry.
Golgappa

$4.9

This is an extremely interesting dessert of the Indian people. The hollowed-out round cakes are fried yellow, crispy, inside filled with peas, mashed potatoes and served with sweet and sour tamarind sauce. Please come here quickly to enjoy!
Fish head curry

$4.67

Fish head curry is cooked by making sure the top of ikan merah (red snapper) with spicy curry water and some vegetables and sour tamarind to create an unmistakable characteristic. It is served with rice, pappadams (a kind of crispy rice cake) and Indian pickles. Hurry up and come here to eat!
Soy yogurt salad

$17.9

Soy yogurt salad is a very familiar dish in Indian daily meals. This dish is made from soy yogurt mixed with cucumbers or with some other root vegetables such as carrots, tomatoes, onions, pineapples, papaya ... will bring you a very good feeling mouth
Chicken Tikka masala

$10.9

Chicken Tikka masala - a traditional and very popular dish of Indians. This dish has a mild flavor but is warm and attractive. Tikka masala chicken has a sauce made from spicy cream, spice and an orange powder. Come here with "FunZone" to experience this traditional dish!
Bimbimbap

$8.9

Bimbimbap is first noticed by the art of color mixing. A bowl of rice bibimbap has from 6 to 7 dishes: white rice, egg yellow, vegetable green, brown meat ... Vegetables are finely chopped cucumbers, carrots, spinach, the price is just cut it, add some lettuce, eggs are rinse or fry together with chopped spiced beef, all these foods will be mixed thoroughly with the sauce made from chili before eating . This mix created the name Bimbimbap.
Gimbap

$1.6667

The name of the dish is very simple, the rice is wrapped in seaweed leaves. In shape, Gimbap "looks" like Sushi - also a Japanese seaweed-wrapped rice dish. But notice more, you will see, Gimbap is usually bigger than Sushi because inside Gimbap there are "kernels" of many different foods. Gimbap is cut in circles with a thinner thickness than Sushi. Gimbap is cut into 12 slices to make it easier for you to eat. Come here to enjoy it!
Soondubu jjigae

$1.72

Sindubu jjigae is a tofu dish stewed with oysters in spicy broth. Soft tofu made with soaked soybeans, dubbed the "beef in the vegetable garden". It is very soft and has a characteristic taste. Please come here quickly to enjoy!
Japchae

$2.15

Referring to Korean cuisine without mentioning Japchae (Korean noodles) is an omission. This dish has many colors from the land of Kim Chi, is a mixture of many vegetables but does not create a boring taste, whereas users will be immersed in the richness of the taste, the feeling will not be bored.
Galbi

$8.6

Galbi is the common name for grilled ribs in Korean cuisine. Many other places, Galbi is made from pork, chicken. In the "Fun zone", Galbi is made from soy sauce beef ribs and baked. When using beef ribs, it is also called "sokalbi" or "soekalbi". If using pork ribs or chicken ribs, it is called "twaechi galbi" or "t’ak galbi". However, because beef ribs are used more often, it is sometimes not just Galbi that implies grilled beef ribs. Please come here quickly to enjoy it!
Kimchi

$2.67

Although there are hundreds of different types of kimchi, most types of kimchi have a strong and spicy aroma. Baechu Gimchi is a typical kimchi among kimchi types. After salting, rinse, mix onions, garlic, chili, ginger, marinated with spices and bring salt. This is considered the best fermented food. It will bring you the spicy, salty, aromatic, ... will definitely make you satisfied!
