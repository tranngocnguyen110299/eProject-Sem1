7 Wonders of the World

Fun Zone

Welcome

Right at "The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park", visitors who do not need to go far also find the world famous works. . Come with your family and friends to experience and take selfies!

Eiffel tower

The Eiffel Tower is the tallest architectural building in France and one of the symbols of Paris. The model is made of glass fiber, has a sophisticated miniature like the real thing, imported and then assembled at the park.

Saint Basil's Cathedral

Model of the cathedral of St. Basil (St. Basil's Cathedral) stands out in the park with colorful colors. This is also one of the most famous buildings of Moscow, Russia and attracts a large number of tourists. St. Basil's Cathedral is one of the most famous churches in the world located in the Southern Red Square, near the Kremlin, Moscow, Russia. St. Church Basil was used to celebrate mass for the public during major festivals held in Red Square.

Statue of Liberty National Monument

Statue of Liberty National Monument is a very famous symbol of America as well as worldwide. The statue with the image of a woman in a robe, right hand holding a torch and the other holding a stone slab with an engraved date of American independence symbolizing freedom and wealth. If you have not had the opportunity to take half a globe to check in, there is a copy in Fun Zone that is no different from the original version.

Sydney Opera House

Referring to Australia, people often think of the model of Sydney Opera House. This is a magnificent building, bearing a unique boat-shaped architecture with windy sails reaching out to the sea. It is not only one of the powerful symbolic images of the modern world, but also the pride of Australians. In The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park also has a work of simulating the Sydney Opera House and attracting many curious young people to live virtually.

The tower of Pisa

The tower of Pisa is the most famous wonder in the world. It is located on an impressive square north of Pisa, Italy. This is one of the most famous tilting works in the world and attracts a lot of tourists to visit. Now you can find it in The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park.

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal architecture is considered one of the most beautiful tombs in India and around the world. The whole building is built of white marble, exquisitely carved in detail, expressing the level of master sculpture architecture at that time. This masterpiece is also modeled at The FUN ZONE Most Entertaining Theme Park.

Tower Bridge

London Tower is a project that combines suspension bridge with lift bridge across the River Thames in London, United Kingdom. Visitors can come here to experience.
