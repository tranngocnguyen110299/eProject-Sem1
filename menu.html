Thrilling game

Young people, healthy, you want to challenge yourself, go through super strong feelings and relieve all stresses in your life ... Let's experience 8 EXCITING HEALTHY GAMES at the Amusement Park FunZone today.

Roller Coaster It has a length of up to 689 m, a 360-degree rotation, speed of 80 km / hour. The challenging journey begins very smoothly, when roller coasters take visitors to a height of 34 m. From there, visitors can completely capture the beautiful scenery of "Amusement Park". $29.79
Sling With the ability to shoot 5 consecutive rounds in 3 minutes, it will make the player experience the feeling of extreme excitement when flying at 31.5m altitude with 360 degree rotation in the air and free fall at a speed of 65km / hours. $45.09
Sun Wheel The giant rotation reaches a speed of 15 minutes per round and can carry up to 384 passengers per turn with 64 cabins. With an impressive height of up to 115m $12.75
Apollo The game helps players experience the feeling of space exploration with higher rotation and zero gravity drop on the legendary Apollo ship. $29.79
Pirate Boat The game was built with the same model as a legendary Viking pirate boat with a capacity of more than 30 people, rotating 1 continuous motion with a height of up to 11m. Suddenly increasing and decreasing the height makes you feel surprised. $29.79
Crazy Wave The game can contain 18 people at once. It will rotate several times in 3 consecutive minutes and change direction. At first it will be very slow but then quickly with 25 rounds / 1 minute. It will give you an interesting and interesting feeling. $45.09
Fly Away The game brings a great sense of pleasure to players with the feeling of flying like superheroes and characters in sci-fi movies. $29.79
Adventure Rope - Climbing Climbing will give you an interesting feeling when you experience and conquer the height. It can be said that this is a sport that not only gives you a sense of conquest, but also trains your health and perseverance. $12.75
